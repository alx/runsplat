Built for Drone Professionals

RunSplat is the 3D reconstruction engine behind future SaaS platforms serving drone operators, surveyors, and inspectors across multiple countries.

🗺

Volumetric Survey

Convert survey flights into accurate 3D Gaussian scenes for stockpile volume estimation, earthwork progress tracking, and site documentation — without LiDAR costs.

  • Millimeter-scale visual fidelity
  • PLY export compatible with GIS tools
  • Multi-flight compositing via video_urls
🏗

Infrastructure Inspection

Inspect bridges, towers, wind turbines, and facades. Share a navigable 3D model with remote engineers — timestamped for damage progression comparison.

  • Shareable browser-based 3D viewer
  • No special software for reviewers
  • Append new flights for change detection
🛰

Aerial Mapping

Produce photorealistic 3D maps of construction zones, agricultural land, or coastal areas — richer than orthophotos, embeddable in any web platform.

  • Sequential or exhaustive COLMAP matching
  • OPENCV_FISHEYE support for wide-angle lenses
  • Up to 300 frames for dense coverage
🎥

Aerial Photography

Turn a single drone flight into an immersive 3DGS scene for real estate, tourism, or media — embeddable in websites with one script tag.

  • WebGL viewer, no plugin required
  • High-quality preset: 60k training steps
  • SPLAT format optimised for web streaming

Building a drone SaaS platform? RunSplat is designed to be your 3D reconstruction backend.
